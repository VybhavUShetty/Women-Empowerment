What we do

Our website aims at providing the scheme initiated by the Government of India as an integrated package of the following services to women and enable them to become economically more viable, independent and raise their socio-economic status. The website will collaborate all the various sectors of scheme and provide a platform for women to become self-dependent. The website consists of the scheme related to Social Welfare and Financial services for women.
















Vision
Promoting social and economic empowerment of women through cross-cuting policies and programmes, mainstreaming gender concerns, creating awareness about their rights and facilitating institutional and legislative support for enabling them realize their human rights and develop to their full potential.







Mission
Promoting social and economic empowerment of women through cross-cuting policies and programmes, mainstreaming gender concerns, creating awareness about their rights and facilitating institutional and legislative support for enabling them realize their human rights and develop to their full potential.
